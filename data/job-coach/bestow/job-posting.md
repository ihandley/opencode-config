## About Bestow

Bestow is a leading vertical technology platform serving the largest and most innovative life insurers. The platform unifies the fragmented, legacy value chain, enabling carriers to launch products in weeks instead of years. Backed by Goldman Sachs, Hedosophia, NEA, Valar, and 8VC.

## About the Role

As a **Staff Backend Engineer** on the IPC (Implementation and Platform Configuration) Engineering team, you will:

- Develop web services using Python + PostgreSQL, Golang, and a Java-based Drools rules engine
- Work with Product Managers, Underwriters, and external partners to power digital life insurance enrollment
- Build new platform capabilities that scale with the business
- Collaborate across Bestow teams to plan and execute the product roadmap
- Integrate services using gRPC, REST, and async messaging patterns
- Operate workloads on Kubernetes in Google Cloud Platform
