## About the Role

Ashby is seeking a Staff Platform Engineer to join their infrastructure team. The role is focused on:

- Building and maintaining platform infrastructure for rapid scaling
- Code-as-infrastructure approach (not snowflake servers)
- Working on DSL-to-SQL compiler optimization
- Implementing automated guardrails for security and privacy
- Supporting developer experience through canary deploys, feature flags
- Defining SLOs/SLIs and implementing corresponding infrastructure
- Building event-driven architecture and data warehouse support
- Ensuring external service communication with retries and circuit-breakers

## Key Responsibilities

1. **Scaling & Infrastructure**: Debug thorny scaling issues and build future-proof platform
2. **Developer Experience**: Ship tools and features that make engineering teams' lives easier
3. **Reliability & Security**: Handle personal/confidential data with high reliability standards
4. **Full Ownership**: Own projects end-to-end with minimal oversight
5. **Problem Solving**: Build systems that engineers want to work with - mature and boring but approachable
